# MIND_MUSCLE_CONNECTION

By harnessing the power of computer vision technology, this system is capable of detecting exercise poses and accurately counting repetitions in real-time. With MMC, you no longer have to worry about improper form or wasted effort during your workouts. This intelligent assistant provides personalized guidance to help you improve your exercise technique, ensuring that you make the most out of every session. By offering real-time feedback and insights, MMC helps you optimize your form, making your workouts more efficient and effective. With a comprehensive library of exercises at your disposal, MMC offers a wide range of options to suit your fitness goals and preferences. Whether you're a beginner or an advanced fitness enthusiast, this AI-powered assistant adapts to your level and provides tailored recommendations to enhance your performance. By leveraging artificial intelligence, MMC promotes healthy habits and physical well-being. Its precise and advanced approach to fitness training ensures that you maintain proper form, minimizing the risk of injuries and maximizing the benefits of each exercise. With MMC as your workout companion, you can embark on a journey to achieve your fitness goals with confidence and precision. The MMC Workout Assistant is an open-source project available on GitHub, providing a platform for collaboration and community-driven improvements.
